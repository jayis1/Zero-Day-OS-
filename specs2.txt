# Cardputer ZERO Additional Launch Information

Based on the official M5Stack launch page, here are the expanded details regarding the CardputerZero's pricing, use cases, and release timeline.

## Pricing Options
*   **CardputerZero Lite:** $59 (Super Early Bird) / $99 (MSRP)
*   **CardputerZero:** $89 (Super Early Bird) / $149 (MSRP)

## Target Audience & Primary Use Cases
*   **Pocket Linux Lab for CLI & Edge AI:** A portable device for connecting to servers via SSH, running Python code, and editing files with CLI tools like Git or Vim. Features lightweight edge AI tools like **OpenClaw** for portable assistance, automation, and testing.
*   **Ultimate Hacking & Cybersecurity Toolkit:** Specifically designed for cybersecurity professionals and hobbyists. It can run security-focused software and connect to useful add-ons like CC1101 and NFC modules for sniffing, testing, and hands-on cybersecurity exploration.
*   **Off-Grid Communication:** Pairs with tools like Meshtastic to keep you connected beyond traditional cellular or Wi-Fi networks, serving as a low-dependency communication companion.
*   **Hardware Tinkering:** Expandable via Grove, M5Units, and custom hardware using SPI, I2C, UART, USB, and GPIO, making it ideal for prototyping and DIY experiments.
*   **Portable Entertainment Hub:** Supports retro gaming with satisfying physical buttons, visual experiments via cameras/HDMI/motion controls, and enjoying music using the built-in speaker and 3.5mm audio jack.

## Software & Ecosystem
*   **Built-in Store:** Allows users to upload their own projects or explore community apps and firmware. 
*   **On-Device Management:** You can switch between apps and firmware instantly right on the device—no PC or reflashing required.

## Kickstarter Timeline
*   **Launch Date:** The campaign is planned to launch on Kickstarter in **mid-to-late May**.
*   **Campaign Duration:** The campaign is expected to run for about a month.
